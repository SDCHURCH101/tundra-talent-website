An Alaska staffing company built for Alaska work.

We are headquartered in Fairbanks with offices in Anchorage and Prudhoe Bay, close to the projects and the people we serve. We know this state, its logistics, and its standards, because we work here too.

Who we are

Skilled trades staffing for construction and the extractive industries

Tundra Talent delivers top-tier skilled tradespeople where performance matters most. We specialize in sourcing and placing professionals with the hands-on expertise to keep operations running, from groundbreakers to gear-turners. For our clients, that means one accountable partner that recruits, vets, mobilizes, houses, and manages the workforce, so their teams can stay focused on the work in front of them.

Our advantage is simple: deep experience in the industries we serve, an uncompromising commitment to safety, modern technology that keeps us a step ahead, and responsive, personalized service from the first call to the final demobilization.
