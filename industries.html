Specialized staffing for Alaska's resource and construction sectors.

We do not try to staff everything. We focus on the industries where remote logistics, safety, and skilled craft decide whether a project succeeds: oil & gas, mining, and construction.

Energy · Upstream · Midstream · Downstream

Oil & Gas

From the North Slope to refineries and terminals, we crew every segment of the oil & gas value chain. Our talent understands remote operations, DOT and OSHA requirements, and what it takes to keep production and pipeline work moving safely.

  • Upstream: drilling, well service, production, and field operations on the North Slope and across Alaska
  • Midstream: pipeline construction, integrity, and gathering and compression facilities
  • Downstream: refinery, terminal, and processing turnarounds and maintenance

Mining

Alaska mining runs on heavy iron and skilled hands in some of the most remote country in the country. We supply the operators, mechanics, and craft trades that keep extraction, processing, and site support running through the season.

  • Heavy equipment operators and haul-truck drivers
  • Field mechanics, welders, and electricians for fleet and plant
  • Processing, camp, and site-support crews for remote operations
