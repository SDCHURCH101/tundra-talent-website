Housing & lodging

Beds secured, so your crew is rested and on time

Lodging is one of the hardest parts of staffing a remote Alaska project. Tundra Talent holds access to man-camp housing in both Deadhorse and Fairbanks, so we can place and rotate crews without scrambling for a place to sleep.

  • Prudhoe Bay / Deadhorse camp access for Slope rotations
  • Fairbanks lodging for staging, training, and Interior work
  • Meals and local transport coordinated with lodging
  • Lodging rolled into one transparent project invoice

Prevailing wage & compliance

Davis-Bacon and certified payroll, handled

On federally funded and prevailing-wage projects, the wage determinations, fringe calculations, and weekly reporting are exactly the paperwork that buries an office. We take it off your plate, so staying compliant is the easy part of the job, not the hard one.

Talk to us about your project
  • Prevailing wage & fringe paid correctly by worker classification, per the project wage determination
  • Certified payroll (WH-347) prepared and filed every week, with signed statements of compliance
  • Accurate classifications and audit-ready records, kept current for the life of the job
  • On-time submissions that keep your project and prime contract in good standing
