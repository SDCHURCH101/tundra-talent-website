Safety is the standard. Technology is the edge.

Two things keep us ahead in Alaska: an uncompromising approach to safety, and the modern systems that make compliance fast, visible, and reliable.

Safety first, always

Every professional, fit for duty and credential-verified

On the North Slope and on the jobsite, there is no margin for cutting corners. Safety is the standard we recruit to, and it is the reason clients trust us with their most remote, highest-stakes work. Every person we place is vetted, screened, and trained to the requirements of the site before they mobilize.

  • DOT & OSHA compliance
  • Pre-placement medicals
  • Drug & alcohol screening
  • Background checks
  • Site-specific orientation
  • Credential & cert verification

Technology that keeps you ahead

The systems behind a faster, cleaner mobilization

Staffing companies that still run on spreadsheets and paper miss expirations, lose time, and create risk. We use modern workforce technology to move faster and give clients real visibility, which is how we stay ahead of the competition in our field.

Digital onboarding

Paperless I-9, tax forms, background checks, and screening scheduling, so a hire can be cleared and ready in record time.

Credential management

Every certification, medical, and clearance tracked in one place, with automatic alerts before anything expires.

Client visibility

See exactly who is on site, who is current, and where each crew member sits in the mobilization pipeline.

Mobile timekeeping

Field time capture that feeds straight into payroll, reducing errors and speeding up the billing cycle.

Data-driven sourcing

A searchable, nationwide talent database lets us match the right skills and certifications to your scope, fast.

One source of truth

Recruiting, credentials, rotations, timekeeping, and payroll connected in a single system, so nothing falls through the cracks.

That experience on the customer's side is our real competitive advantage.
